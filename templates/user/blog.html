Top 10 Emergency Tips Everyone Should Know

Published on June 15, 2025 • By Saran Ambulance

1. Stay Calm and Assess the Situation

In any emergency, staying calm helps you think clearly. Assess the scene for danger before taking any action.

2. Call for Help Immediately

Dial 108 or your local ambulance number. If in Saran, call 8008280020 for instant ambulance booking.

3. Know Basic First Aid

Learn how to stop bleeding, perform CPR, treat burns, or help someone who's choking. These skills can save lives.

4. Keep Emergency Contacts Saved

Save hospital, ambulance, and close family contacts under 'ICE' (In Case of Emergency) on your phone.

5. Maintain a First Aid Kit

Always keep a stocked first aid kit at home and in your vehicle. Include bandages, antiseptic, gloves, and scissors.

6. Recognize Signs of Stroke/Heart Attack

Fast action during a heart attack or stroke is critical. Know symptoms like chest pain, numbness, or speech trouble.

7. Avoid Moving Seriously Injured People

Unless there's an immediate threat, do not move victims with suspected spinal injuries.

8. Use the Heimlich Maneuver for Choking

It’s a simple technique that can be used if someone’s airway is blocked by food or objects.

9. Understand Emergency Codes

Know emergency codes at your workplace or public buildings like fire alarm signals, evacuation maps, etc.

10. Stay Updated with CPR & First Aid Training

Attend refresher courses regularly. What you know today could save a life tomorrow.
